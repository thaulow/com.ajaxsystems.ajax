Connect your Ajax security system to Homey and bring your intrusion, fire, and water protection into your smart home.

This app connects to your Ajax hub through a proxy server and receives real-time alarm events directly over your local network using the SIA DC-09 protocol.

What you can do:
- Monitor your alarm state — armed, disarmed, or night mode
- Receive alarm events in real-time — fire, intrusion, water leak, tamper, and more
- Arm and disarm your Ajax system from Homey or through flows
- Monitor motion sensors, door/window contacts, smoke detectors, water leak sensors, glass break detectors, and more
- Control smart plugs and relays
- Track battery levels, signal strength, and online status for all devices
- Trigger Homey flows on alarm events and system state changes

How it works:
The app uses two channels simultaneously. Cloud commands (arm, disarm, device status) go through a proxy server using your Ajax account. Alarm events are received instantly over your local network via the SIA DC-09 protocol — the same standard used by professional monitoring stations.

Supported devices:
- Hub 2, Hub 2 Plus, Hub Hybrid
- MotionProtect, MotionProtect Plus, MotionProtect Outdoor, MotionCam, CombiProtect
- DoorProtect, DoorProtect Plus
- FireProtect, FireProtect Plus, FireProtect 2
- LeaksProtect
- GlassProtect
- HomeSiren, StreetSiren, StreetSiren DoubleDeck
- Socket, Relay, WallSwitch, LightSwitch
- Button, DoubleButton, SpaceControl
- LifeQuality

Requirements:
- An Ajax hub reachable on your local network
- A compatible Ajax proxy server
- Your Ajax account email and password
